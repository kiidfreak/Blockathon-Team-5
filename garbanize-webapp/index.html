THE GBN PROGRAM

Our Organisation has formed a community programme that organises cleanup days from funds collected by funding programmes. This will effectively be done by the users of the platform with the organisers being the top garbage collectors and the best tenants/land lords. This program aims to move from a local to an international movement whereby a specific day/month or week will be set up with a theme to curb poor waste management and promote good ennvironment practices.

-Rewarding the community through tokens and digital assets.
